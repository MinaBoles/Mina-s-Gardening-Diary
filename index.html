Welcome to Mina's Gardening Diary website

A journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step

In 2018 I had a back injuiry and this resulted in me being a wheelchair bound. It is incredible how we can adapt to new life styles. Having lost the ability to walk ,and all function in my left side, I could not do the things I loved anymore such as; walking, cycling and running. So, I took up gardening as a hobby after spending just over 4 months in hospital.

Everything was extremely hard at the beginning; accessibility issues , handling and carrying isssues , reaching the ground to sow, and above all I had zero experience in gardening.

I started following some gardening accounts on Instagram , and it seemed that everyone had green fingers. But soon after, I was hit by the reality and most of my plants died. I almost gave up but with several attempts and perseverance I succeeded and things started to flourish.

I learned the lesson; that I will never ever give up. If I can do it , so could you!
